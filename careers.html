Process

Our hiring process.

1. Apply — Send us your resume and a brief note about why you're interested in District.

2. Initial Chat — A 30-minute call to learn more about each other and discuss the role.

3. Skills Assessment — A practical exercise relevant to the role (take-home or live session).

4. Team Interviews — Meet the team you'd work with and discuss how you'd collaborate.

5. Offer — If it's a great fit, we'll extend an offer and welcome you to the team.
