Creator Accelerator

An intensive program designed to fast-track emerging creators' growth. Over 12 weeks, selected creators receive hands-on mentorship, strategic guidance, and resources to scale their channels and build sustainable businesses.

  • 1-on-1 mentorship from successful creators
  • Customized growth strategy and roadmap
  • Content strategy and optimization workshops
  • Brand partnership opportunities
  • Access to District's creator network

Mentorship Program

Connect with experienced creators who can guide you through your journey. Our mentorship program pairs emerging creators with established mentors for ongoing guidance, feedback, and support.

  • Monthly 1-on-1 mentorship sessions

  • Access to exclusive workshops and events

Creator Scholarships

We believe creator opportunities should be accessible to everyone. Our scholarship program provides financial support and resources to underrepresented creators who show promise but lack access to resources and networks.

  • Financial support for equipment and production
  • Free access to District programs and workshops
  • Mentorship and networking opportunities
  • Brand partnership facilitation
  • Ongoing support and guidance
