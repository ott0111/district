Answers to common questions about District for creators, brands, and community.

For Creators

Creator questions.

Fill out our application form with information about your channels, audience, and goals. Our team reviews applications and reaches out to creators who are a good fit for our roster.

We work with creators across various niches including tech, gaming, lifestyle, fashion, education, and business. We look for creators who are focused on long-term career building and sustainable growth.

We work on a commission basis for brand partnerships we secure. There are no upfront fees or long-term contracts. We only earn when you earn.

We're not just an agency — we're an ecosystem. District Talent is connected to District Community, District Partnerships, and our other divisions, giving creators access to comprehensive support beyond just brand deals.

For Brands

Brand questions.

Contact our partnerships team with details about your campaign goals, target audience, and budget. We'll match you with creators who align with your brand and campaign objectives.

We work with brands across technology, gaming, fashion, beauty, food & beverage, finance, health & wellness, and more. If you have a target audience, we can help you reach them.

Every creator in our network is vetted for content quality, audience engagement, and brand alignment. We have content review processes, contract protections, and ongoing monitoring to ensure your brand is represented safely.

Campaign timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Typically, we need 2-4 weeks for planning and creator matching, followed by campaign execution which can range from 2-8 weeks depending on the campaign.

For Community

Community questions.

Fill out our community application form. We review applications to ensure a good fit for our community culture. Once approved, you'll receive an invitation to join our Discord and other community channels.

District Community is currently free to join. We may introduce premium tiers in the future with additional benefits, but the core community will remain accessible.

Members get access to our Discord community, exclusive workshops and events, creator resources, collaboration opportunities, and early access to District tools and programs.

General

General questions.

District was founded in 2024 with the vision of building comprehensive infrastructure for creators to build sustainable careers.

We're always looking for talented people who share our vision. Check our Careers page for current openings and reach out if you're interested in joining the team.

For general inquiries: hello@district.co. For talent applications: talent@district.co. For brand partnerships: partnerships@district.co. For press: press@district.co.

District is a remote-first company with team members across multiple time zones. We embrace distributed work and collaborate primarily through digital tools.
