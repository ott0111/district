Team

Founder bio.

Jordan Chen — Founder & CEO

Jordan Chen is a former creator turned entrepreneur who founded District after experiencing firsthand the challenges creators face in building sustainable businesses. With a background in both content creation and business development, Jordan recognized the need for comprehensive creator infrastructure and built District to fill that gap.

Under Jordan's leadership, District has grown from a vision to a comprehensive ecosystem supporting creators across talent management, community, software, and beyond.

About

Company description.

Short Description (100 characters):

District — Building the next generation of creators.


Medium Description (250 characters):

District is an ecosystem that helps creators build careers, businesses, products, and communities. From talent management to software, community to commerce — everything under one brand.


Full Description:

District is building the infrastructure creators need to build sustainable careers and businesses. Founded in 2024, District operates as a comprehensive ecosystem including District Talent (creator management), District Community (creator networking), District Partnerships (brand collaborations), and upcoming divisions in software, apparel, events, and programs. Everything lives under one brand, unified by a single vision: helping creators build real careers that last.
